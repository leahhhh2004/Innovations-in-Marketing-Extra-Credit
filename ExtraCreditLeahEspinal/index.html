About Antonio Reynoso

Antonio has always stood up to powerful interests. He fought the corrupt Brooklyn political machine. He also took on corporate polluters and kicked ICE (Immigration and Customs Enforcement) off Riker's Island.

He never backs down from important fights for New Yorkers. He delivers real results for working families.

Now, he's running for United States Congress to take on those same fights in Washington. He will challenge corporate interests and fight against Trump’s overreach and MAGA (Make America Great Again) extremism. He’ll use all tools to abolish ICE, tax the rich, and stop the genocide in Gaza. He also aims to fully fund healthcare, childcare, and housing to help working families thrive.

Issues Antonio is Fighting For

Taking on ICE

ICE (Immigration and Customs Enforcement)is terrorizing our communities. It rips families apart and targets immigrants in New York. Antonio has never stood by while that happens. He stopped NYPD's (New York Police Department) coordination with ICE on Rikers Island.

This helped thousands of immigrants get work permits, find legal help, and access resources to build their lives here. Antonio will work in Congress to end ICE. He will use every tool to protect immigrant families.

Housing Crisis

Developers and speculators are profiting, while working families are leaving New York. That’s not an accident; it’s a rigged system. Antonio has tackled the system directly. He fights for affordable housing in every neighborhood.

He pushes bold plans to truly solve the crisis, not just manage it. In Congress, he will work to repeal the Faircloth Amendment. He'll invest in public housing and ensure working people can afford to stay in their communities.

Pollution

For too long, corporate polluters have treated working-class communities like dumping grounds. Antonio grew up breathing that reality, and he’s spent his career fighting back. He made important changes. They cut pollution.

They hold companies accountable. They protect frontline workers. In Congress, he will fight against polluters. He'll back clean energy and protect everyone's right to clean air and water.
